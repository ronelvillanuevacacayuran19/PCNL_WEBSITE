BSA
Provides a strong foundation in financial accounting, auditing, and taxation. Equips students with the skills necessary to become Certified Public Accountants and financial experts. Emphasizes analytical skills and ethical practices in financial management.
BSBA
Focuses on developing leadership, entrepreneurial, and management skills. Prepares students for various roles in the business sector, emphasizing practical training and real-world applications. Offers specializations in areas such as Human Resource Development Management, Financial Management, and Marketing Management.
BSCRIM
Concentrates on law enforcement, criminal justice, and forensic science. Aims to mold graduates into competent professionals dedicated to public safety and crime prevention. Provides insights into the legal system and the societal impacts of crime.
BEE
Prepares future educators with knowledge of teaching methodologies and child development. Focuses on creating effective learning environments and fostering student engagement. Emphasizes the importance of nurturing the next generation of learners through innovative teaching practices.
BSIT
Focuses on essential skills in programming, networking, database management, and emerging technologies. Prepares students for careers in the digital landscape, emphasizing both theoretical knowledge and practical application. Encourages innovation and adaptability in a rapidly changing technological environment.
